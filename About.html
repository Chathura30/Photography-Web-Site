About Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. He has a deep passion for capturing the natural world through his lens.

With years of experience, Malcolm has mastered the art of photography. His portfolio includes stunning images of the rugged Scottish landscape, natural wildlife, and coastal birds.

Passion for Photography

Malcolm's photography journey started as a way to share the breathtaking beauty of Scotland's landscapes. Over time, his skills expanded to cover various photography genres, including weddings, portraits, and special events.

Every shot he takes reflects his dedication to preserving precious moments in time. His keen eye and attention to detail ensure that each photograph captures the essence of the subject.
